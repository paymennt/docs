understanding user permissions

There are four levels of access in the Paymennt app:

Admin: Change all settings, manage users, send payment links, creating an event, adding and removing products from the online store, seeing sales related to affiliate accounts, scanning QR codes, and changing account and bank settings.

Branch : send and monitor secure payment links, view branch sales (including all terminals assigned to branch). No ability to change any account settings, ideal for multiple branch managers.

Terminals: send and monitor secure payment links, view only their terminal sales. No ability to change any account settings

Read-only: view sales and download statements only, ideal for accounting partners and financial monitoring,

Scan only: For terminals that have events enabled, this access level allows user to scan and verify event tickets at the time of the event.
